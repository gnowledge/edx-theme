## About Section ##

About Online Courses at metaStudio

metaStudio.org is a platform that supports the development of online courses as well as a massive open online course (MOOC) delivery platform.

The gnoweldge lab of Homi Bhabha Centre for Science Education is running a customized instance of the Open EdX platform at this site. The site is currently intended for the use of undergraduate students of NUSSD program.

## FAQ Section ##

FAQs

What browsers are recommended for use with OpenEdX?
The latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, and Apple Safari. (The site is currently not optimized for mobile use.)
