HONOUR CODE PLEDGE
By enrolling in an courses.metastudio.org course, I agree that I will: Complete all mid-terms and final exams with my own work and only my own work. I will not submit the work of any other person. Maintain only one user account and not let anyone else use my username and/or password. Not engage in any activity that would dishonestly improve my results, or improve or hurt the results of others.
